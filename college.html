University of Michigan Ann Harbor

University of Michigan is ranked 22 in the nation and has an amazing dermatology program. It is about a four hour drive outside of Chicago and is located in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Their campus has beautiful scenery and has amazing student life. Yearly out of state tuiton is $57,273. There are seven dining halls to choose from which are an aditional cost as well. The requirements to apply to University of Michigan are $75 application fee, highschool transcript, school report, teacher evaluation, SAT or ACT scores, and a completed common app essay. UMICH excels in academics as well as athletics. They are known for their football team which is ranked best college team in the country. UMICH is one of my top dream schools and I can hope one day I will attend.


Stanford University

Stanford is ranked 3 in the nation and, similar to UMICH, has an amazing dermatology program. Stanford is located 35 miles south of San Francisco, California. The weather is warm all year round. The yearly tuition out of state is $62,484. Stanford has nine dining halls. For the fall quarter it is about $2,649 for a dining hall membership. The requirements to apply to Stanford University are $90 application fee, SAT or ACT scores, school report, counselor and two teachers reccomendation, official transcript, and a midyear transcript. Stanford ranks 12 nationally in dermatology. They are known for their excellent academics. I can see myself being set up for success by attending Stanford, my dream school.
