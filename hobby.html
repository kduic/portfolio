Golf

I have been golfing for a little over a year. When I made the Lane team I took the sport seriously, trying to find time to practice. Even though the game is very frustrating, I still have continued to play hoping it is something I can always enjoy. When I practice it is something I enjoy doing to get things off my mind because if you have a head full of thoughts, you won't be able to play your best. I didn't play much the summer going into high school, but I started practicing more and more at the end of summer to make the team. By not practicing all summer I realized if I did, I could have been much better. Now I love playing the game and play to have fun.
