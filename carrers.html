Dermatology

When I am older I would like to be a dermatologist. I have always had an interest in skin and how it effects people's appearance and confidence. Dermatologists spealize in skin, hair, and nails. They work with patients who have skin cancer to people who just need their moles checked. Dermatologists are in a high demand and make around 310k-410k per year. To qualify for being a dermatologist you must have spent four years in medical school and earn a degree. Then go into three years of residency. People who work in dermatology must have knowledge of most medicine. I see myself in this career because I have an interest and enjoyment in this job. I would like to do a job I love and can help others feel their appearance suites them. Helping people discover they may need treatment for skin cancer or other skin conditions is something I would like to be a part of my job.

Income Rates

The average yearly salary for dermatology is 310k-410k but it can vary depending on location.

Knowledge Required

To become a dermatologist you must know the basics of the human body. You will study anatomy, biochemistry, and medicine. Studentd also learn how to deal with patients, take medical history, examine patients, and make a diagnosis.

Schooling

To become a certified dermatologist you must go through four years of medical school and three years of residency. Overall it is about 11-12 years of schooling including an undergraduate program.
